About SRM ACM SIGCHI

SRM ACM SIGCHI is the official student chapter which focuses on building innovation based on both technology and human experience within SRM Institute of Science and Technology.

We are more than just a technical club. We are a multidisciplinary innovation hub

Our chapter focuses on human-centered design, UI/UX principles, design thinking, research methodologies,
